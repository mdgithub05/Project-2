💡 Key Business Insights Recommendations
🥇 Best Category: Office Supplies Highest avg margin at 43.3%. Increase shelf space & promotional priority for this category.
⚠️ Furniture Under-performing Margin of only 19.9% with high inventory days. SKU-level audit needed for Bookcases & Tables.
🏷️ Cap Discounts at 20% Margin drops from 37.8% (0% disc) to 17.4% (50% disc). Every 10% discount = ~4% margin erosion.
📦 Clear Dead Stock (60+ days) Items sitting 60+ days show lowest margins. Clearance campaigns can free working capital.
🌍 Central Region Leads Central delivers highest total profit (₹4.56L). Replicate its strategy in South region.
☀️ Summer = Peak Technology Technology hits ₹4.97L sales in Summer. Align marketing & stock replenishment accordingly.
